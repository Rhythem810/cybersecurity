Frequently Asked Questions

What is penetration testing?

Penetration testing is a simulated cyberattack on your systems to identify vulnerabilities before attackers can exploit them.

How do I secure my network?

Network security involves measures like firewalls, encryption, and intrusion detection systems to protect your network from unauthorized access and attacks.

What is multi-factor authentication?

Multi-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security by requiring more than just a password to access an account, such as a fingerprint or a code sent to your phone.
